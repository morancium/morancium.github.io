Inter-IIT Vital Extraction Challenge: Revolutionizing Healthcare with Computer Vision

May 1, 2024 AI & Computer Vision
Silver Medal - Inter-IIT Tech Meet 11.0

Introduction

The Inter-IIT Vital Extraction Challenge was a prestigious competition at the Inter-IIT Tech Meet 11.0, where teams were tasked with developing a computer vision model to extract vital signs from ECG monitor images. Our team from IIT Roorkee achieved remarkable success, securing a Silver Medal with a solution that achieved 93% accuracy.

Problem Statement

The challenge required participants to develop a system that could accurately extract vital signs from images of ECG monitors. This is a critical task in healthcare, as it enables the digitization of patient data from various medical devices, improving record-keeping and analysis.

Technical Approach

Our solution leveraged several advanced computer vision and deep learning techniques:

  • YOLO-based Detection: Used for accurate localization of vital signs on the monitor
  • Data Augmentation: Implemented to enhance model robustness
  • Noise Reduction: Applied preprocessing techniques to improve image quality
  • Optimization: Achieved inference speed of 2.3 seconds on Google Colab CPU

Key Features

  • High accuracy (93%) in vital sign extraction
  • Robust performance across different monitor types
  • Fast inference time suitable for real-time applications
  • Minimal hardware requirements

Technical Implementation

The project was implemented using:

  • Python as the primary programming language
  • PyTorch for deep learning implementation
  • OpenCV for image processing
  • YOLO for object detection

Challenges and Solutions

We faced several challenges during development:

  • Image Quality: Addressed through advanced preprocessing techniques
  • Real-time Processing: Optimized model architecture for speed
  • Accuracy Requirements: Implemented ensemble methods and careful hyperparameter tuning

Results and Impact

The project demonstrated significant achievements:

  • 93% accuracy in vital sign extraction
  • Silver Medal at Inter-IIT Tech Meet 11.0
  • Potential for real-world healthcare applications
  • Contribution to medical data digitization

Future Applications

This technology has numerous potential applications in healthcare:

  • Automated patient monitoring systems
  • Digital health record management
  • Remote patient monitoring
  • Medical research data collection

Conclusion

The Inter-IIT Vital Extraction Challenge was a significant milestone in our journey of applying AI to healthcare. Our solution not only achieved high accuracy but also demonstrated the practical potential of computer vision in medical applications. The success of this project highlights the importance of interdisciplinary approaches in solving real-world healthcare challenges.
